Features

Discover everything you can do on Therapy Connected.

For Visitors & Clients

  • Browse Therapists: Search, filter, and sort therapists by specialty, location, credentials, and more. View detailed therapist profiles with bios, specialties, credentials, and contact info. See therapist availability and professional tier.
  • Contact Therapists: Securely contact therapists via email directly from their profile card.
  • Read the Blog: Explore articles written by therapists on mental health, wellness, and therapy tips. Search and filter blog posts by topic, author, or keyword.

For Therapists

  • Create & Manage Your Profile: Build a professional profile with photo, credentials, specialties, tagline, and bio. Update your information anytime.
  • Dashboard: View statistics: profile visits, contact count, and last viewed date. Manage your blog posts (create, edit, delete, and view all your posts).
  • Blog Authoring: Write and publish blog posts to share your expertise. Tag posts for discoverability.
  • Onboarding Wizard: Guided onboarding to help you set up your profile step-by-step.
  • Subscription & Payments: Choose a professional tier and manage your subscription securely.

Platform Features

  • Modern, Accessible Design: Calming pastel palette, responsive layout, and accessible navigation.
  • Advanced Search: Powerful search and filtering for both therapists and blog content.
  • Secure & Private: Data privacy and secure contact forms.
  • Mobile Friendly: Fully responsive for all devices.
  • Easy Navigation: Clean navbar, search modal, and clear links to all major sections.
